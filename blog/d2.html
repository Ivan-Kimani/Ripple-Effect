Everything is Falling Apart...

Raising kids can be a challenging and emotionally draining task and unfortunately there is no manual. Every child comes with their own set of challenges. Then add to the fact that you must go to work and face challenges there as well. Sometimes it can feel like you are drowning, and you have no strength left in you to stay afloat. You just want to leave them for a while, quit your job, recoil in a corner, cry your eyes and heart out. However, you must be brave for you, for them and keep that job. Asking for help is the bravest thing you can do. Asking for help is not giving up, it is refusing to give up.
