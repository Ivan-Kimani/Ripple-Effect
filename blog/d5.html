Put The Phone Down !!

Son: Mom you are not listening, you are always on your phone... Me: Nope, I respond with a stern look at this child accusing me. Son: If you are listening, what did I say? I have a guilty and sheepish look on my face as this human is looking at me waiting for an answer. I put the phone down quickly and ask, what were you saying? Son: Never mind Is the response I get. Ouch! that hurts! If you are a parent and have heard such a statement , often, it's time to listen. If you do not there will come a time when you have time to listen and their answer will hurt even more. Mum am okay. This time your gut feeling is telling you they are not okay but you have been shut out. It's time to put down the phone and listen. Where there is a Will, there is a way.
