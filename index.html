About Me

Coach Sandra -The single mum Coach

I am Coach Sandra, an experienced and certified single-mum coach with over 10 years of dedicated expertise in supporting single mums through their transformative journeys. My personal journey has been one of triumph over tribulations, a path carved by mistakes and growth, and a testament to the power of resilience. As a single parent who has faced daunting obstacles, wrestled with darkness, and emerged stronger, I am dedicated to guiding you towards a life of empowerment and fulfillment. Let me help you embrace your true potential and build a life that radiates resilience and empowerment.


Why Coach Sandra?

I am not just a coach. I am your ally, your confidante, and your source of unwavering support. Through my own experiences, I have navigated the tumultuous waters of single parenthood, surmounted heartache, and triumphed over seemingly insurmountable odds. I have raised three incredible children while juggling work, personal growth, and the myriad of roles life has thrown my way.


Coach Sandra's Jouney

My coaching philosophy centers on the belief that every single parent possesses an innate ability to rise above adversity. Through the right mindset and attitude, you can emerge from life's trials stronger and more resilient than before. I provide a safe and nonjudgmental space for you to explore your fears, dreams, and aspirations. Together, we'll uncover the tools you need to rebuild, transform, and thrive.


What can you expect from the Pogram?

• Resilience Building: Discover how to overcome setbacks and transform challenges into steppingstones towards a brighter future.
• Mindset Mastery: Learn to cultivate a mindset that fosters growth, positivity, and a deep sense of self-worth.
• Parenting Support: Navigate the complexities of single parenting with strategies to enhance communication, connection, and understanding with your children.

• Self-Care Strategies: Prioritize your well-being and embrace self-care as an essential component of your journey.

• Goal Achievement: Define and pursue your goals with confidence, guided by a personalized roadmap to success.

• Community Building: Connect with a network of like-minded single mothers who share your aspirations and challenges. Ready to embark on a transformative journey of self-discovery, empowerment, and growth? I invite you to join our program. Together, we'll unleash your potential and create a life that celebrates every facet of who you are.

Testimonial

I met Sandra (to me Wanjiku) when my life has been turned upside down and everything was painted a very venomous black. Lost my income during covid 19 season which threw me in total dependence on my husband and that did not go well as he lost his job 6 months later.
She played and prayed a very major role in my turn around. I was slowly sinking into depression and life was slowly turning useless. God used her to paint the brick picture into a colored mirage and I slowly turned from a low self esteem to believing myself all over again and regaining my self worth.
Wanjiku to me is a combination of so many things. A prayer warrior, a no nonsense girl who gives you as it is, very honest and yet able to connect emotionally with a lot of people. That's Wanjiku to me.
